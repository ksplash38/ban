Branchless Banking Benefits

We put the banking services you need as close as your smartphone, tablet or computer.

Saying no to branches means saying yes to more of what you want and need from a bank.

Because we're an all-digital banking platform, we spend less on expensive branches – that's how we can offer higher interest rates†. And we offer the peace of mind that comes with knowing we’re a Member of FDIC, and have the highest possible A+ rating from the Better Business Bureau.

Banking as close as your smartphone, tablet or computer. And, as close as one of our over 85,000 surcharge free ATMs**

That’s more ATMs than the top five U.S. banks own, combined^^. It’s easy to get cash just about anywhere you are, anytime you need it. Use our ATM locator in the Sussex County Bank App‡ or online to find your nearest surcharge free ATM in the U.S. or internationally.

Your security is our first priority

From advanced security protocols like one-time passwords for new devices, 128-bit encryption and biometric log in, to the microchip embedded in your Sussex County Bank MasterCard® Debit Card, we implement and offer a number of services, policies and procedures to keep you safe. In addition to protecting your Sussex County Bank account and banking activity, we even also offer free software, IBM® Security Trusteer Rapport®, that will help protect you and your web activity beyond Sussex County Bank.

With MemoryAlerts‡, we'll remember so you don't have to

MemoryAlert notifications‡ give you the peace of mind of knowing if you have enough in your account for day-to-day spending, and enough to build long-term memories. There are two easy ways to set your MemoryAlert push notifications, texts, or emails‡ after you've enrolled for Sussex County Bank digital banking. 1) From the App, log in and go to the App Settings to enable and customize your push notifications‡. 2) Or, from your computer, log in and go to "Manage My Sussex County Bank." Click "Add and manage your account notifications" to set up and customize SMS Text and email alerts‡.

So many more digital banking conveniences. For your life.

Open your account from your smartphone or computer. Apply in as little as 10 minutes. No more finding an open branch. No more mailing an application and waiting. Get started now.

 

24/7 banking the way you want it. Once you’ve opened your account, download our free App‡ for your iPhone®, iPad®, Android™ phone or tablet, or Amazon Fire®, or manage your banking right here on our website. Take a look at supported browsers for the best web experience.

 

Secure mobile‡ login without needing to remember your password. Eyeprint ID™ login performs a simple scan of your eyes' unique blood vessels with your smartphone’s camera. Or, use your smartphone’s fingerprint ID‡‡.

 

The info you need – whenever, wherever. Just swipe down the App’s log-in screen to check your balance‡ — no actual log-in required. And set up your own custom MemoryAlert notifications — for transactions, deposits and when you go below any specific balance you choose — through push notifications, texts or emails‡.

 

Get moneyback rewards when you shop. Use your Sussex County Bank App‡ or online banking to find and activate moneyback offers for the kinds of places you like to shop and dine1. Then use your Sussex County Bank MasterCard® debit card to pay, and the cash rewards you’ve earned will be directly deposited into your Sussex County Bank account1. Learn more about Purchase Rewards.

 

Keep your wallet in your pocket when you pay – use your Sussex County Bank MasterCard Debit Card with Apple Pay® or Samsung Pay®†† on your compatible devices for in-store and in-app‡ purchases. Learn more about setting up Apple Pay and Samsung Pay††.

 

No cash to pay someone? No problem. With PopMoney® built into the Sussex County Bank App, it’s as easy as texting or emailing‡. You can send and receive money with just about anyone without cash or checks. Free transfers take place in just 3 business days, and you can get next business day transfers for just $3.00.

 

No paper, no searching, no filing. Log in to your account and click the online statements link to view, sort, search, save and print the last 18 months of free online statements. All while reducing the risk of ID theft and mail fraud by having your statements stored safely within online banking.

 

Pay bills without checks, envelopes or stamps. And with complete control. With free Bill Pay you can pay anyone in the U.S.. by scheduling a single, recurring or future-dated payment.
