What is Sussex County Bank?

Sussex County Bank is a national branchless all-digital banking platform. As a division of Republic Bank & Trust Company, Sussex County Bank provides its account holders the peace of mind that comes with knowing it is a Member of FDIC with the highest possible A+ rating from the Better Business Bureau.

How safe is branchless banking with Sussex County Bank?

As part of an established, trusted bank with over $4 billion in assets, we utilize advanced security technology to protect your financial data, and deposits up to $250,000 are FDIC-insured. We have also been recognized as an A+ rated company by the Better Business Bureau.

back to top

What products does Sussex County Bank offer?

We currently offer our high-earning interest checking account, the EarnMore^ Account, and a second-chance checking account, Memory Builder. In the future we plan on expanding our product line to include loan products and other deposit products.

back to top

Does Sussex County Bank have any locations?

Sussex County Bank does not have any physical branches. Because Sussex County Bank is a national branchless all-digital banking platform, we spend less on expensive branches – that's how we can offer higher interest rates†. In other words, saying no to branches means saying yes to more of what you want and need from a bank. And with your Sussex County Bank MasterCard® Debit Card you have access to over 85,000 surcharge-free ATMs** across the country and internationally – more ATMs than the top five U.S. banks own, combined^^.

Where are Sussex County Bank ATMs located?

Our network of over 85,000 surcharge-free ATMs** is spread across the United States and internationally – more ATMs than the top five U.S. banks own, combined^^. To find your nearest ATM, use the ATM locator on this site or in the Sussex County Bank Apps‡ for iPhone®, iPad® or Android™ phones and tablets.

What do I need to have to apply for and open an account with Sussex County Bank?

  1. Your U.S. Social Security number
  2. A valid driver's license, State ID or Military ID
  3. Current address, and previous home address if you have lived at your current address less than two years
  4. An email address where we can send your account information
  5. A credit or debit card, or a U.S. checking or savings account to fund your new account (we require a minimum opening deposit of $50.00)

If you are opening a joint account, you will need items 1 through 3 above for each applicant.

If I fund my account from another account at a different financial institution, what is the process and how soon will I begin earning interest?

2 to 3 days after you provide the funding account information, we will make two small test deposits into (and two equal withdrawals from) your funding account. Once these test transactions are received, you will need to log back into your Sussex County Bank application and provide the two deposited amounts when prompted. If you open an EarnMore^ account, you will begin earning interest on the effective business date which we receive your verification of these test transactions. The MemoryBuilder Second Chance Checking Account is a non-interest bearing checking account.

How long will it take to receive my Sussex County Bank MasterCard Debit Card?

You should receive your card approximately 3 to 5 business days after account funding.

How can I get a replacement card?

To replace your card, log in to your account on the web and click “Manage My Sussex County Bank,” or give us a call at 1-877-757-3400. Delivery is free for standard 3 to 5 business day delivery, but you can request expedited 2-day and next day delivery for an additional charge. Expedited requests for 2-day must be made by 2:00PM Eastern, and for next-day must be made by 11:00AM Eastern..

Will I receive checks?

How do I begin using Sussex County Bank mobile‡ and online banking?

After opening your account, you will be provided your account number which you will use to enroll in mobile and online banking. It’s easy to get set up with Sussex County Bank Apps‡ for iPhone, iPad or Android phones and tablets. Click here to find the Sussex County Bank App‡ you need, download the App and click “Sign Up”. You can also enroll in online and mobile‡ banking with a new username and password by clicking the "Login/Enroll" button at the top of every page on this site, and then clicking “New User”.

Why do I need to create two different username and password combinations?

For your security, you will be required to set up a different username and password than you used when applying for your account to begin using Sussex County Bank mobile‡ and online banking. To ensure your security, this username and password combination should be different than the combination you used for your account application.

What if I've forgotten my password (or I've been locked out of the system)?

How frequently is my Sussex County Bank account information updated?

Transactions are displayed within mobile‡ and online banking in real-time, which means that most transactions are reflected immediately on your account. ATM and point-of-sale transactions using your Sussex County Bank MasterCard Debit Card are visible as soon as they are received by the bank.

How do I make a deposit?

Adding money to your account is simple. You can deposit checks using our mobile deposit1 feature, set up a direct deposit, or set up transfers from other financial institutions. We also offer Popmoney‡, which is a popular way of transferring money between your Sussex County Bank account and just about anyone who has an email address or mobile phone number.

back to top

How do I deposit cash?

As an all-digital banking platform, we currently do not accept cash deposits. You can deposit the funds into another bank account and then transfer the funds to your Sussex County Bank account using our external transfer feature on our online banking website. At no time should you ever mail cash to us.

back to top

How do I set up Direct Deposit of my payroll or other monthly income?

After your Sussex County Bank application has been approved, you will receive Sussex County Bank’s routing number and your account number. If your employer participates in a direct deposit program, simply provide this information to the human resources or payroll department at your company, and your direct deposit will usually begin within thirty days. To set up any other monthly direct deposits, such as Social Security, you will need Sussex County Bank’s routing number and your account number, along with our bank name when you contact the department or office that manages monthly deposits.

After I have signed up online for Bill Pay, how long does it take for me to start paying bills on mobile‡ or online?

Once you have completed your Bill Payment application located under the Bill Payment tab in online banking, you will be able to start Bill Payment within 1-2 business days. You will be notified by e-mail once your application has been processed. Click here for more information on Bill Pay.

How do I withdraw my money?

Sussex County Bank provides you a MasterCard Debit Card that enables you to shop anywhere MasterCard is accepted. In addition, with access to over 85,000 surcharge-free ATMs**, you’ll have easy access to your cash. We also offer free online bill pay, Popmoney‡, and other external transfer options available through the web and full-featured iPhone and Android mobile Apps‡.

What is the Purchase Rewards Program?

Purchase Rewards lets you earn cash rewards by using your Sussex County Bank MasterCard debit card to purchase merchandise and services. The program is available to all Sussex County Bank MasterCard debit cardholders. Through this program, you’ll receive targeted offers to receive rewards on purchases based on how you shop. Offers are based on how you currently shop to ensure the offers you receive are relevant. It may take up to 60 days after you start using your debit card for offers to become available. The more you use your Sussex County Bank MasterCard debit card, the more chances you will have to receive relevant offers.  See the Purchase Rewards FAQs for more information.

Are wire transfers possible with my Sussex County Bank account?

You are welcome to wire funds into your account but we currently do not offer outgoing wire services.  To accept a domestic incoming wire, please inform the sender to use our incoming wire routing number: 091017620, along with your Sussex County Bank account number. If you wish to accept an international incoming wire, please contact us at (877) 757-3400 for instructions.  Incoming wires occurring within the first 30 days of account opening will be refunded the wire fee.  Please see our Fee Schedule for more information.  Other ways you can move money in and out of your account include external transfers and PopMoney®.
